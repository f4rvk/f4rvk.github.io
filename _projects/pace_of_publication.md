How fast are psychologists publishing, and has that pace changed over time? This project uses bibliometric methods to examine trends in publication speed across psychology, and compares it with economics, political science and sociology.

Presented as a poster at the Association for Psychological Science (APS) 2026 annual convention in Barcelona, and as a blitz talk at SIPS Online 2026.
