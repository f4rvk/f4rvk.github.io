How fast are psychologists publishing, and has that pace changed over time? This project uses bibliometric methods to examine trends in publication speed across psychology, with an eye toward what acceleration (or lack thereof) might imply for the quality and depth of the research being produced.

Presented as a poster at the Association for Psychological Science (APS) 2026 annual convention in Barcelona, with a blitz talk submitted to SIPS 2026.
