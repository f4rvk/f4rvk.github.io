Does experiencing moral outrage change the way people seek out and consume information? This project, completed as my [MS thesis](https://etda.libraries.psu.edu/files/final_submissions/34159) at Penn State, examined the relationship between moral outrage and subsequent information-seeking behavior, asking whether outrage narrows or broadens the range of information people pursue.
